Frequently Asked Questions



What are the requirements for renting a car?
To rent a car, you generally need to be of a minimum age of 18 and hold a valid driver's license. Some locations may have additional requirements, such as a minimum driving experience or the need for an international driver's license. Make sure to review the specific terms and conditions on our website or contact our customer support for detailed information.
What types of cars do you offer for rent?
We offer a wide range of vehicles to cater to different needs and preferences. Our fleet includes compact cars, sedans, SUVs, luxury vehicles, vans, and more. You can filter the available options on our website based on your desired vehicle type, size, features, or other preferences.
What is included in the rental price?
The rental price typically includes the base cost of the vehicle for the specified duration, but additional fees and charges may apply. These may include taxes, insurance coverage, fuel, mileage limitations, airport surcharges, and optional extras such as GPS navigation or child seats. It's important to carefully review the pricing details and any applicable terms and conditions during the booking process.
Is insurance included in the rental?
Basic insurance coverage is often included in the rental price. However, it's important to carefully review the insurance terms and limitations. Additional insurance options, such as collision damage waivers or supplemental liability coverage, may be available for purchase to provide further protection during your rental period.
Do you offer long-term rental or leasing options?
Yes, we offer long-term rental and leasing options for customers who require a vehicle for an extended period, typically beyond a month. These options often come with discounted rates and flexible terms. Contact our customer support or check our website for more information on long-term rental or leasing arrangements.
Are there any penalties for returning the car late?
Late return penalties may apply if you exceed the agreed-upon return time without prior notification or arrangement. These penalties can vary depending on the rental duration and the terms of your booking. It's recommended to inform us in advance if you anticipate any delays or need to extend your rental period to avoid potential penalties.
